About Me

I'm Killua Zoldyck. I come from a family of assassins. Unlike my family, however, I do not wish to be an assassin. I want my life to mean more than killing and getting paid. Besides, it's too easy.

I decided to go to the Hunters' Exam for a challenge and to get out of the house. Along the way, I met a kid about my age (12). It's rare for me to find people my age with the amount of skills I have, and after talking for a while during the first phase of the exam, we started to become friends-- a hard thing to come by for assassins.

That was when I realized what I most wanted in this world: a friend. It wasn't long until we became just that. After the exam, we traveled the world, and mostly I tagged along, but I didn't mind; being there with him was enough for me. Not only did we physically grow stronger and learn super cool fighting techniques, but we grew to know each other. I'm just glad that I didn't turn out how my family wanted, and now I can live a life knowing what having someone you care about is like.
